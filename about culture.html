About culture of Telangana

The Culture of Telangana in India has a cultural history of about 5,000 years.
Telangana State has long been a meeting place for diverse languages and cultures.
It is known as "South of North and North of South". It is also known for its Ganga-Jamuna
Tehzeeb and the capital Hyderabad is known as a miniature India.
